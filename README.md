### 📝 A Little Bit About Me | 關於我

Hi! I'm Christina. I consider myself a highly reflective person who enjoys figuring things out in the AI and cognitive science space, cherishing the process of learning along the way. 
*(你好！我是 Christina。我是一個喜歡內省與反思的人，目前正在 AI 與認知科學領域摸索學習，享受著每天進步一點點的過程。)*

- 🔭 **Currently:** Research Assistant at **AI-Cooperatives, Academia Sinica**, Taiwan. 
  *(目前在台灣中央研究院 AI-Cooperatives 擔任研究助理)*
- 🧠 **Research Focus:** My background is deeply rooted in **fMRI research**, with hands-on experience in **EEG projects**. I'm currently fascinated by the potential of open, non-invasive Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) applications.
  *(我的專業背景是 fMRI 研究，同時也具備 EEG 專案經驗。目前對於開放且非侵入式的腦機介面 (BCI) 應用充滿熱情與興趣！)*
- 🌱 **Learning:** Exploring the intersection of human cognition and AI collaboration, while trying to write better code every day.
  *(專注於探索人類認知與 AI 協作的交集，並努力把程式碼寫得更好。)*
